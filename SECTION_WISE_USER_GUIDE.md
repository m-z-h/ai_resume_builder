**A**: Yes! Certifications and Additional Information are optional. Just click "Next Section" to skip.

**A**: Your resume is auto-saved. Just log back in and continue from where you left off.

### Q: Can I change my template later?
**A**: Yes! You can change templates in the Preview section.
