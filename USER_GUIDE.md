# Step-by-Step Resume Builder - User Guide

## Getting Started

### Accessing the Builder
1. Log in to your account
2. From the Dashboard, click on **"Step-by-Step Builder"** card
3. You'll be taken to the resume creation wizard

## Creating Your Resume

### Step 1: Personal Details
Fill in your basic information:
- **First Name** and **Last Name** (Required)
- **Email** and **Phone** (Required)
- **Address** (Optional)
- **LinkedIn** and **Website** (Optional)

Click **"Save & Next"** to proceed.

### Step 2: Professional Summary
Write a brief professional summary or use AI:
- **Manual Entry**: Type your own 2-3 sentence summary
- **AI Generation**: Click **"Generate with AI"** button
  - AI will create a professional summary based on your details
  - You can edit the generated text
  - Click again to regenerate if needed

Click **"Save & Next"** to proceed.

### Step 3: Work Experience
Add your work history:
1. Click **"+ Add Experience"**
2. Fill in:
   - Job Title (Required)
   - Company Name (Required)
   - Start Date and End Date
   - Job Description

**AI Assistance Available:**
- Fill in Job Title and Company
- Click **"Generate Description"** button
- AI creates professional bullet points
- Edit as needed

Add multiple experiences by clicking **"+ Add Experience"** again.

Click **"Save & Next"** to proceed.

### Step 4: Education
Add your educational background:
1. Click **"+ Add Education"**
2. Fill in:
   - Degree (Required)
   - Field of Study
   - Institution (Required)
   - Start Date and End Date

Add multiple degrees if applicable.

Click **"Save & Next"** to proceed.

### Step 5: Skills
List your professional skills:
- Enter skills separated by commas
- Example: "JavaScript, React, Node.js, Python, SQL"
- Skills will appear as tags below the input

Click **"Save & Next"** to proceed.

### Step 6: Projects
Showcase your projects:
1. Click **"+ Add Project"**
2. Fill in:
   - Project Name (Required)
   - Project URL (Optional)
   - Technologies Used
   - Description

**AI Assistance Available:**
- Fill in Project Name and Technologies
- Click **"Generate Description"** button
- AI creates a professional description
- Edit as needed

Click **"Save & Next"** to proceed.

### Step 7: Certifications (Optional)
Add professional certifications:
1. Click **"+ Add Certification"**
2. Fill in:
   - Certification Name (Required)
   - Issuing Organization (Required)
   - Date Earned
   - Verification URL

This section is optional. Click **"Save & Next"** to proceed.

### Step 8: Preview & Design
Customize your resume appearance:

**Design Options:**
- **Font Family**: Choose from 6 professional fonts
- **Font Size**: Small, Medium, or Large
- **Color Theme**: Select from 5 color schemes
- **Layout**: Single-column or Two-column

**Preview:**
- See your complete resume with selected design
- All sections are displayed as they will appear

**Download:**
- Click **"Download PDF"** for PDF format
- Click **"Download Word"** for editable DOCX format

Click **"Save & Finish"** to complete.

## Tips for Best Results

### Using AI Generation
1. **Provide Context**: Fill in basic information before using AI
2. **Review & Edit**: Always review AI-generated content
3. **Regenerate**: Don't like the result? Click again for new content
4. **Customize**: Edit AI content to match your voice

### Writing Effective Content
1. **Be Specific**: Use concrete examples and numbers
2. **Action Verbs**: Start bullet points with strong verbs
3. **Achievements**: Focus on results, not just duties
4. **Keywords**: Include industry-relevant terms
5. **Concise**: Keep descriptions clear and brief

### Design Choices
1. **Professional Fonts**: Inter, Roboto, or Open Sans work well
2. **Readable Size**: Medium font size is recommended
3. **Conservative Colors**: Blue or Gray for traditional industries
4. **Layout**: Single-column for ATS compatibility

## Editing Your Resume

### Navigating Sections
- Click on any step in the progress bar to jump to that section
- Green checkmarks indicate completed sections
- Make changes and click **"Save & Next"**

### Saving Changes
- Changes are saved when you click **"Save & Next"**
- You can leave and return anytime
- Your progress is automatically tracked

## Downloading Your Resume

### Available Formats
1. **PDF**: Best for submitting to employers
   - Professional appearance
   - Preserves formatting
   - Widely accepted

2. **Word (DOCX)**: Best for further editing
   - Editable format
   - Can customize further
   - Compatible with most systems

### Download Tips
- Save your resume before downloading
- Download both formats for flexibility
- Test PDF on different devices
- Keep a backup copy

## Troubleshooting

### AI Generation Not Working
- Check your internet connection
- Ensure you've filled in required fields
- Try again after a few seconds
- Contact support if issue persists

### Can't Save Section
- Verify all required fields are filled
- Check for error messages
- Ensure you're logged in
- Try refreshing the page

### Preview Not Updating
- Click **"Save & Next"** to update preview
- Refresh the page if needed
- Check that changes were saved

### Download Issues
- Ensure resume is saved first
- Check browser download settings
- Try a different browser
- Clear browser cache
